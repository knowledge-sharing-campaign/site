HISTORY OF KSC

1. Background

The Knowledge Sharing Campaign (KSC) creates a platform that brings together two groups of people: the Know Can Teach those who have some experience and would like to share their knowledge) group and the Dont Know Can Learn (those who would like to learn, but do not have the opportunity) group. KSC originated from a simple idea, which turned out to be its slogan: Bring, Share, and Learn. We believe that if these two groups come together, agree to bring and share the minimum required resources that facilitate learning, then KSC becomes an intersection between humanity and technology that provides a great platform to promote excellence in education. KSC is based on two key principles that can be derived from the following quotes.
